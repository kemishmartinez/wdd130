Down the River - Water Adventures!

Welcome to Down the River! Our goal is to provide you with the best white water experience possible. For over 30 years, our family-owned business has been guiding thrill-seekers and families down the region's most exciting rivers. With our elite team of certified guides, we are completely dedicated to making your river adventure safe, memorable, and absolutely unforgettable.

History

Down the River Water started floating the river in 1995 with just two rafts and a passion for the great outdoors. Over the last three decades, we have grown into one of the premier outdoor guiding services in the country. We are a family owned and operated business that remains dedicated to providing you with the best experience possible. From training top-tier swiftwater rescue guides to preserving our beautiful natural waterways, our history is built on a deep love for the river and a commitment to our community.

Adventure Awaits You

The river is calling! Whether you are looking for a relaxing or a family float adventure, we have the perfect trip waiting for you. Spaces fill up fast during the summer season, so grab your paddle, gather your crew, and book your next legendary river trip with Down the River!
